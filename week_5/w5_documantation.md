## 1.1 Define the problem statement!
    Develop a Python programm for a small cafe that automatically calculates a customers bill 
    based on the quantity of coffee, tea and sandwiches ordered and prints receipt.
---
## 1.2 What are the inputs?
    - Customer name
    - Quantity of coffee
    - Quantity of tea
    - Quantity of sandwiches 
---
## 1.3 What are the outputs?
    - Customer name
    - Quantity of each item
    - Total bill (RM)
    - Receipt
---
## 1.4 What would be the typical proccess flow?
    - Enter customer name.
    - Enter quantity of coffee
    - Enter quantity of tea
    - Enter quantity of sandwich
    - Calculate the total bill
    - Print the receipt
---
## 1.5 What are the constrains?
    - Quantities must be whole numbers
    - Quantities cannot be negative
    - Prices are fixed
---
## 2.0 How do we decompose the Problem?
    1. First we we need to get the customer's name
    2. Than we need to get the quantity of items that need be purchased.
        - Quantity of cofee
        - Quantity of tea
        - Quantity of sandwiches
    3. After we got all the details needed we will calculate the "TOTAL BILL".
    4. And last but no least we need to show the output in a receipt.
---

    Input sandwich quantity
